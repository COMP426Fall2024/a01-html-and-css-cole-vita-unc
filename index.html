Who is KMP?

Ketan Mayer Patelicorn, a professor of legendary status at UNC, is known for his unparalleled ability to teach complex computer science topics with ease, humor, and a touch of mythological flair.

The Vegas Fantasy Football Debacle

KMP is in a high-stakes fantasy football league in Vegas. Once he drafted an all-kicker team, claiming it was a "meta-strategy." Despite the skepticism of seasoned gamblers, his team, "The Toe-Tal Dominators," somehow won every game by freak coincidences—like when all his opponents' quarterbacks were mysteriously out with food poisoning. Rumor has it, KMP now holds a lifetime ban from most Vegas sportsbooks, but no one can quite prove how he pulled it off.
